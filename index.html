Episode Summary

Core Theme

Most businesses fail at growth marketing not due to poor execution, but because of flawed personnel strategy. They expect one person or agency to handle all marketing aspects, leading to inefficiency and burnout.

Key to Success

The episode teaches that real growth happens when businesses separate brand-building ("magnet") and conversion-driving ("engine") roles, creating true momentum.
